Visual

Easily organize your ideas visually with board space. Connect your ideas in powerful ways, with links, hierarchical pages, and endless widgets.

Free

All features are completely free to use. Not to mention, the entire app is open source. Break free from proprietary productivity services that hold your data hostage.

Customizable

Want to change the color of a list or note? Dislike the color of a specific button or menu? No problem. You'll be able to fine tune everything to your liking.

Collaborative

Easily work with others in shared workspaces, all in real time. Get group projects done effectively with assigned and alternating tasks.
